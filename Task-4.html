Lykan HyperSport

The first hypercar created by W Motors, the Lykan HyperSport, catapulted the company to international stardom when it took center stage in Universal Studios’ Furious 6 Furious 7. It was featured as the hero car in the movie and is the most expensive car ever to be featured in The Fast & The Furious Franchise.

The “Lycan” is the most advanced species of Wolves in mythical legends. Hidden under an elegant and luxurious mask, the Lycan can transform at any given time should the need arise to show its power and leadership. Written with a “k”, W Motors Lykan is an exceptional Sportscar hypercar with an elegant yet agile look, able to transform at any given time into a beast, the most advanced of them all.
